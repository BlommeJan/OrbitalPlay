About OrbitalPlay

OrbitalPlay is a fitness-inspired gaming platform that unites custom-built JavaScript games with an innovative controller. Accessible online, the games feature dynamic themes and skins for a unique experience. Combined with a custom controller that delivers real-time feedback and intuitive controls, OrbitalPlay offers a fully immersive way to merge fitness and fun.
